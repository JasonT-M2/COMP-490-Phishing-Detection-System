PHISHING DETECTION SYSTEM

Advanced Email Threat Analysis

About This Project

The Phishing Detection System is designed to help users identify potentially malicious emails before they cause harm. Phishing attacks attempt to trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data.

This platform analyzes email content and looks for common phishing indicators including suspicious links, urgent language, spoofed domains, and other patterns frequently used in cyber attacks.

By pasting an email message into the analyzer, the system evaluates the content and provides feedback on whether the message may be safe or potentially dangerous.

Our goal is to increase cybersecurity awareness and provide a simple tool that helps individuals and organizations avoid phishing scams.

Tip: Always verify sender addresses, avoid clicking unknown links, and never share sensitive information through suspicious emails.
